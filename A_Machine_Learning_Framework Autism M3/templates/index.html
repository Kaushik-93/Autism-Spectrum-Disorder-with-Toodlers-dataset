About Autism Spectrum Disorder

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). Autism is a complex and lifelong condition that affects a person's ability to communicate, interact with others, and understand social cues.

(ASD) what it is?

Social difficulties: People with autism may have trouble with social interactions, such as difficulty making eye contact, understanding social cues, or engaging in reciprocal conversations.

How to identify Symptoms

Having difficulty sustaining attention in tasks or play activities.

Being easily distracted by irrelevant stimuli.

Frequently making careless mistakes in schoolwork or other activities.

Struggling to follow through on instructions or tasks.

Having difficulty organizing tasks or activities.

Avoiding or disliking tasks that require sustained mental effort.

Frequently losing necessary items, like school supplies or toys.

Being forgetful in daily activities.

Recursive Feature Elimination

Feature selection in RFE (Recursive Feature Elimination) is a technique used to select the most relevant features from a given dataset. RFE is an iterative process that starts with all the features and eliminates the least important features at each iteration until a specified number of features remains.
